A little about me...
I'm 29 years old, I live in Valencia and I'm a student of 3D Animation, video games and interactive environments. My two life companions are called Trébol and Ura, a 14 year old mixed breed dog and a 6 year old female dog. We love to enjoy nature, whenever I can, we go hiking with my friends or travel with the family to rural places. I like to go skating and I also practice yoga. I take life with humor, I am a very clownish, cheerful and talkative person. I have always been interested in learning all kinds of subjects. Before doing this training, I studied communicative mediation and worked as an assistance dog trainer. The perfect excuse to end up living with 5 dogs at home. I am a creative, enthusiastic and curious minded person. I started this cycle in September 2021 and I only use the computer to watch Netflix, and do some work for uni or insti in Word or PowerPoint. In this blog you will find work done during the training. The main purpose of this portfolio, is to have a showcase of them. I try to make them original, high quality, and fun. Of course, don't expect to find animations like Pixar, or productions at the level of Game of Thrones.
